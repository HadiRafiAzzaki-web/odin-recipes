Watermelon Lemonade

This is a simple recipe for watermelon lemonade. Garnish each glass with a slice of watermelon and a sprig of mint.


Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place watermelon into a blender; cover and puree until smooth.
  3. Bring sugar and 1/2 cup water to a boil in a saucepan over medium-high heat until sugar dissolves, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat; stir 3 cups of cold water and lemon juice.
  4. Divide ice into 12 glasses; scoop 2 to 3 tablespoons of watermelon puree over ice, then top with lemonade.
  5. Gently stir before serving.
